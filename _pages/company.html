Company

Halo&Co is Korea's first OTA-focused NDC aggregator and an API-based B2B SaaS provider specializing in airline distribution technology. Originally spun off from Tidesquare's NDC business division, Halo&Co launched Korea's first commercial NDC integration in 2019 and has since built a track record of direct connections with global carriers like Finnair and Air France KLM, leading NDC adoption in North Asia.
