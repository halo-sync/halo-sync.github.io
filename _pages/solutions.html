HaloSync is...

HaloSync is a next-generation NDC SaaS platform designed to simplify airline distribution, automate operations, and help travel sellers scale with confidence.

Simplicity at scale

Simplify the complexity of NDC content with a unified, streamlined API architecture.

Smart operations, automated

Automate booking, ticketing, and servicing workflows to boost efficiency and reduce manual overhead.

Seamless airline connectivity

Connect directly to leading airlines through PolarHub — fully NDC-certified and scalable.

Intelligence in control

Manage fares, offers, and commissions dynamically with our intelligent back-office system, Albus.

PolarHub

API Connectivity hub:
Integration with multiple airline NDC APIs, unified access through PolarHub API

Simplifies and expands access to the NDC content ecosystem, enabling seamless connectivity and rich content availability.

Albus

Platform control & admin:
Airline sales management, route control, promotions, settlement & reporting

Delivers superior control and actionable insights, empowering airlines and platform managers to maximize performance and profitability in the NDC era.

Luna

B2B Portal service:
Flight search, booking/ticketing, post-booking services, full NDC content access

Provides a comprehensive and user-friendly portal for B2B users, enabling smooth NDC operations that enhance efficiency and deliver superior customer service experiences.

Built on certified technology

Our platform is officially certified and supported by global standards
- so you can focus on growth, with peace of mind.
