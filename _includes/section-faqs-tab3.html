What features are provided by the platform?

We support features such as instant ticketing, cancellations and refunds, ancillary purchases, and reservation changes. Please note that available functionalities may vary by airline. For details, please refer to Solutions > Our solutions & airline integration matrix.

Do you display all fare types (e.g. branded fares, Private fares, corporate fares)?

Yes, we provide all types of fares. We offer airline-specific branded fares, corporate fares, and special discounted fares.

What ancillaries do you offer?

  • Commonly, baggage and pre-seat reservations are available. There may be additional ancillary services specific to each airline, and we plan to add common services such as in-flight meals, lounge access, and Wi-Fi in the near future.
  • Service availability may differ depending on the airline. Please visit Solution > Our solutions & airline integration matrix for more information.
